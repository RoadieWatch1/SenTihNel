# Account Deletion - SenTihNel

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the SenTihNel app
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:
- Your email address and profile information
- Your location history
- Fleet memberships
- SOS alert history
- Check-in records
- Subscription data

## Data Retention

- Account data is deleted immediately upon request
- Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion
